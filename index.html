ABOUT US

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) Satara is a residential school in the Satara district of Maharashtra, India. As part of the larger Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya system, JNV Satara follows the same principles and objectives in providing quality education to students, especially from rural areas. Here is some specific information about JNV Satara: 1. **Location:** Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Satara is situated in the picturesque district of Satara in the state of Maharashtra, known for its natural beauty and historical significance. 2. **Establishment:** JNV Satara was established as part of the Navodaya Vidyalaya System, which aims to provide education to talented students from rural backgrounds. These schools were founded as per the National Policy on Education in 1986. 3. **Admission Process:** Admission to JNV Satara, like all Navodaya Vidyalayas, is based on the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST). This competitive entrance exam is open to students in the 5th grade, and successful candidates are granted admission to Class VI. 4. **Free Education:** JNV Satara, like other Navodaya Vidyalayas, provides free education to its students. This includes not only tuition but also boarding, lodging, textbooks, and uniforms.
