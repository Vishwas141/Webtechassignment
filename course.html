Our Courses

Facilities We Offer

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are a system of government-funded schools in India that provide quality education to students from rural areas, with a focus on promoting national integration and providing opportunities to talented children.

Hostels

JNVs are fully residential schools, and students live on the campus. The residential nature of JNVs helps students develop a strong sense of discipline and responsibility.

CBSE Education

JNVs are known for their commitment to quality education. They follow the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum. Students are provided with a conducive study environment, with well-equipped classrooms and libraries.

Sport and Art

JNVs emphasize the overall development of students, including their physical and extracurricular activities. Sports like cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, and other games are encouraged, and many JNVs have sports facilities.
