About Us

We are the best residential schools is India

  • Location: JNV Satara is situated in the picturesque region of Satara, which is known for its natural beauty and is located in the western state of Maharashtra.
  • Establishment: The JNV in Satara was established as part of the Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme, which aims to provide quality education to talented students from rural areas. It was likely established in accordance with the guidelines and principles that govern all JNVs in the country.
  • Admission: Like all JNVs, JNV Satara admits students through a merit-based selection process. Students are selected based on their performance in the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST), which is conducted annually.
  • Curriculum: JNV Satara follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum, which is a standardized and well-recognized educational system in India.
  • Residential Facilities: JNV Satara is a fully residential school, and students live on the campus. The hostel facilities are designed to provide students with a comfortable and conducive environment for learning.
  • Faculty: The teaching staff at JNV Satara is typically well-qualified and dedicated to the welfare and education of the students.
  • Extracurricular Activities: JNVs, including JNV Satara, encourage students to participate in various extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural events, debates, and quizzes. These activities help students develop a well-rounded personality.
  • Community Integration: JNV Satara, like all JNVs, plays a role in promoting national integration by bringing together students from diverse linguistic, cultural, and socioeconomic backgrounds. This fosters an environment of unity in diversity.
  • Regional Significance: JNVs like JNV Satara have a significant role in the education of rural and underprivileged students. They provide access to quality education and better opportunities for students who may not have had such prospects otherwise.
